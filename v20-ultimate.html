Your Voice Has Been Leaking Your Mental State Your Entire Life.

This isn't science fiction — it's neuroscience.

When you're stressed, anxious, or depressed, your autonomic nervous system alters how your vocal cords vibrate. Micro-changes in pitch, jitter, shimmer, and spectral energy — signals you can't control, can't fake, and can't suppress.

Researchers have known this for decades. What's new is a 244M-parameter model trained on 35,000 patients that can read these signals from 25 seconds of natural speech.

Voice biomarkers are involuntary neurological signals — they bypass the cognitive filters that cause people to minimize, deny, or not recognize what they're experiencing. The signal is always there. In every conversation, every call, every meeting.
